Akan names

This website helps you know your birth day using the Ghanian Akan name.

The Akan people of ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and in order in which they were born.
This day's names have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person.Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, or an ancestor's middle name.

Take a look at some of the Akan Names and find out what your's really is.
